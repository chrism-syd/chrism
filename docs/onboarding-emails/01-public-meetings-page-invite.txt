Email label
- Public meetings page invite

User flow that triggers this email
- A visitor opens a public council meetings page.
- The council has no upcoming published General or Executive meetings.
- The page shows the blank-state message and a small form asking for Name and email address.
- Someone enters a council officer's name and email address and clicks Send email.
- Chrism sends a secure email link.
- After the recipient clicks the link, they land in signed-in council onboarding.

Intent
- Turn a blank public page into a soft onboarding entry point.
- Give the council a path into setup instead of a dead end.
